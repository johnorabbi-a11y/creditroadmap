FAQ

Does this affordability checker calculate how much I can borrow?

No. It does not calculate borrowing limits. It only gives an educational view of monthly commitment pressure.

What does the affordability profile mean?

Comfortable, Moderate and Stretched are broad indicators based on commitments compared with estimated gross monthly income.

Should I use take-home pay as well?

Yes. Gross salary is only a rough input, so checking take-home pay can give a clearer view of monthly budget pressure.

Is this personalised advice?

No. It is general UK credit education only and does not replace qualified financial or debt advice.
