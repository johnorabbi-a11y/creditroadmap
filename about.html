About

About Credit Roadmap UK.

Credit Roadmap UK is a plain-English planning tool for people who want to understand common credit-file factors before making future applications.

What the site does

The site helps users think through issues such as CCJs, defaults, missed payments, electoral roll records, credit utilisation and recent applications. The roadmap generator turns broad answers into general next steps, recommended reading and a staged plan.

The aim is to make credit preparation less confusing. It is not designed to replace actual credit reports, product documents, lender criteria or qualified advice.

What the site does not do

Credit Roadmap UK is not financial advice, debt advice, mortgage advice, legal advice or credit broking. It does not recommend lenders, arrange products, assess eligibility, take applications or guarantee approval.

The site is not affiliated with any lender, broker, credit provider or credit reference agency.

How to use it responsibly

Use the guidance as a starting point. Check your statutory credit reports, confirm that records are accurate and consider your affordability before applying for any product. If repayments are difficult, seek free debt help before taking on more credit.

The launch checklist gives a simple order for using the site carefully.

How content is written

Content is written in cautious UK-focused language. It avoids promises such as promises of approval or score improvement. The editorial standards page explains the approach in more detail.
