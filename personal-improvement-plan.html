How to use it

Treat the plan as a checklist, not a prediction.

The plan helps prioritise practical steps such as report checks, electoral roll updates, application pauses, utilisation reduction and payment stability. Lenders may consider their own criteria and this tool does not promise outcomes.

FAQ

Does the personal improvement plan predict credit score changes?

No. The plan is an educational action checklist only and does not predict credit scores, lender decisions or outcomes.

Can I save my plan?

Yes. Saved plans use localStorage in your browser only. There is no backend account or server storage.

Can I export the plan?

Yes. The export button downloads a plain text summary for your own records.

Can this promise acceptance?

No. Lenders use their own criteria and this tool does not promise acceptance or any particular result.
