FAQ

How long does a CCJ usually stay on a UK credit file?

CCJs typically remain on standard credit reports for six years from the judgment date.

Does the six-year anniversary promise removal?

No. This calculator gives an estimated anniversary only. You should check your credit reports and public record information.

Does paying a CCJ remove it immediately?

Payment may update the CCJ as satisfied, but a visible CCJ can still affect lender decisions while it remains on credit files.

What should I check if the date looks wrong?

Check the judgment date, court record, satisfaction status and all credit reports. Seek qualified support if you are unsure how to deal with a judgment.
