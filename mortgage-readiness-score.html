FAQ

Does this mortgage readiness score predict whether I will get a mortgage?

No. It is an educational readiness check only. Mortgage lenders use their own criteria and may consider affordability, deposit, income, credit history and documentation.

What factors does the score use?

It uses broad credit preparation factors: CCJ status, defaults, missed payments, electoral roll status, credit utilisation and deposit percentage.

Can a larger deposit help mortgage readiness?

A larger deposit may help some applications look less risky, but it does not remove the need for affordability checks or a full lender assessment.

What should I do before applying for a mortgage?

Check all credit reports, reduce avoidable balance pressure, keep payments current and consider speaking to a qualified mortgage adviser if your situation is complex.
