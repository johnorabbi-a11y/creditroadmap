Privacy

Privacy Policy.

This page explains how Credit Roadmap UK is designed to handle information in a simple static website context.

Roadmap answers

The roadmap questionnaire runs in your browser. It does not require an account and does not submit your answers to a lender, broker or credit reference agency. The site does not make a credit application for you.

Credit checks are not carried out

Using the site does not perform a hard search or soft search. The site does not access your credit reports. You should check your own statutory credit reports directly if you need accurate personal data.

Hosting and basic logs

Like most websites, the hosting provider may process basic technical logs such as IP address, browser type, requested page and time of access for security and reliability. Credit Roadmap UK does not use this information to make lending decisions.

Cookies and analytics

The current static site does not require cookies for the roadmap generator to work. If analytics or additional services are added later, this policy should be updated to explain what is collected and why.

External parties

Credit Roadmap UK is not affiliated with lenders, brokers, credit providers or credit reference agencies. The site does not sell your roadmap answers to those organisations.

Do not enter sensitive details

The tool only needs broad answers. Do not type account numbers, passwords, National Insurance numbers, full debt reference numbers or other sensitive personal details into the site.
